Welcome

"Connection established. All signals are functioning optimally. Test passed—"

You were jolted awake by the signal alert. Blinking your eyes open, you found yourself in a deep blue void. Floating text bubbles drifted within your line of sight.

"Where am I?"

Rubbing your eyes, fragments of memory surfaced. It was 2025, and you were a starfarer—an interstellar adventurer. Last month, you had ordered the latest neural host-jump device from Planet Y's X Corporation. Rumor had it that by connecting to the device and entering a specific neural port code, you could link directly to another person's "neural host"—their innermost consciousness! Skeptical but intrigued, you had casually entered the number 1021, followed the instructions to close your eyes, and then… wound up here.

"This is insane! So this is someone's inner world? But… whose?

Curiosity burning, you reached out and touched one of the floating text bubbles. Words slowly materialized————
